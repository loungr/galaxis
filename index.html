persona

/pər-sō′nə/ noun

A character presented by an individual to satisfy the needs of a situation. Each individual has a few Personas, not necessarily the ones that are presented or perceived by others.

world

Every Persona belongs to a cluster of Worlds. Each World has its own norms and unique contents.

galaxis

Galaxis lets you create a different Persona to participate in each of your public or private World, thus replicating a universe of Worlds that you engage in.
